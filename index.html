We at mentoons are here to mentor through cartoons! Focusing on concepts such as social media de-adiction, we use psychology-based modules and cartoons to influence and change maladaptive behaviour in your child. Mentoons has got your back, whether you're a parent looking for a holistic yet fun growht environment for your 6 year old or even if you're a 20-year old looking for career guidance!



Based on the “9 Human Intelligences” Theory , Our workshops are designed and delivered by Psychologists. Our fun sessions will help children learn about thoughts, interactions, reactions, making friends, self awareness and more!

A fun camp for your kids where they can learn, enjoy and make friends!

  • 🌸 Interactive sessions helps with building connections with your peers
  • 🌸 Journal writing helps to acknowledge their thoughts.
  • 🌸 Books, games and videos helps build their drive for participation.
  • 🌸 Helps your child engage and mingle with their friends

Dealing with teenage years can be difficult. Our teen camp deals with.

  • 🌸 Growth Mindset- How to improve your skills with hard-work.
  • 🌸 guide them on concepts like good touch-bad touch, social media, and cell phone addiction.
  • 🌸 Journal writing to help deal with emotions.
  • 🌸 Building healthy relationships with parents.
  • 🌸 Dealing with body and hormonal change

Our aim is to have a positive impact on children by first positively impacting their parents

  • 🌸 Our workshops create a fun learning environment for parents.
  • 🌸 Exciting activities are designed which will help in parent-child interaction.
  • 🌸 great opportunities where parents can refresh their skills.
  • 🌸 understand how to manage areas like pampering kids.
